Welcome to Honduras Volunteer

Foreign language skills and international experience are and will remain an essential building block for professional success. A stay abroad is therefore a worthwhile and necessary investment in the future. Social skills and language skills are becoming increasingly important in today's professional environment and global circumstances.

Through a volunteer assignment or internship in Honduras, you develop your emotional and social intelligence and make a contribution to better understanding between two cultures.

To ensure that your stay abroad is a complete success with many interesting and positive experiences, you should leave nothing to chance - we are happy to organize an unforgettable volunteer assignment or internship in Honduras for you.

Areas of Deployment

Honduras Volunteer strives to organize an experience-rich and unforgettable stay in Honduras for you. We select the area of deployment (the project) based on your interests and wishes. As a small family organization, we are able to respond individually to your interests.

Some examples of possible areas of deployment:

  • Social work in various social institutions
  • Kindergarten & School
  • Medicine (hospital, clinic, physiotherapy)
  • Counseling & Education (e.g., AIDS education, counseling, treatment)
  • Working with disabled people
  • Animal project & Animal protection
  • Sports project

Spanish Lessons

So that you can quickly communicate and converse in Honduras, we recommend that you acquire basic knowledge of Spanish before departure. If you would like to deepen your Spanish skills on site, we will be happy to arrange private Spanish lessons for you. However, this is not included in the participant fee.

Important to know: In Honduras, "Spanish lessons" does not mean teaching Spanish grammar and spelling (this is not considered very important by the Honduran population :-). Rather, it is about improving communication skills in Spanish.

Costs for mandatory internships (colleges/university) on request

Each additional week costs CHF 125. e.g., 5 weeks: CHF 1575

Included:

  • Individual consultation before departure
  • Organization and coordination of the volunteer work assignment
  • Stay with a host family* with meals (half board)
  • Work assignment in desired area / project
  • Transport from the airport to the host family (in Honduras)
  • On-site support / orientation on site

* In exceptional situations, another form of accommodation is also possible.

Not included:

  • Health and accident insurance
  • Travel to and from Honduras
  • Transport host family – project – host family
  • Cancellation costs
  • Excursions and trips / leisure activities / pocket money
  • All costs related to a visa: only for stays longer than 3 months
