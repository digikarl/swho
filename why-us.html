Why SWHO?

At Honduras Volunteer, we are dedicated to providing meaningful volunteer experiences while fostering cultural exchange between Switzerland and Honduras. Our organization offers unique advantages to ensure your time abroad is both impactful and transformative.

  • In-depth knowledge of Honduran culture and local conditions
  • Years of experience in organization and support
  • Professional and reliable pre-departure advice and assistance
  • Personal guidance on the ground in Honduras
  • Customized programs tailored to your needs
  • Enhancement of career prospects and personal development

About SWHO

SWHO stands for SWitzerland HOnduras. It is recognized in Switzerland as a non-profit association promoting cultural exchange.

A Message from Our Founder

I am Swiss, and my first experience volunteering in Honduras was in 2005. I fell in love with the country and its people, who welcomed me warmly without hesitation or prejudice. Since then, I have been visiting Honduras regularly.

My experience as a volunteer inspired me to establish SWHO, and I am convinced that SWHO provides the best conditions for a successful stay. Nearly 20 years of our existence confirm our success and vision.

Our Commitment to You

We believe that international volunteer work should be a mutual exchange that benefits both the volunteers and the communities they serve. Our programs are designed to:

  • Engage in meaningful work that truly makes a difference
  • Immerse you in the authentic Honduran culture
  • Support your personal and professional development
  • Ensure your safety and comfort throughout your stay
  • Create lasting connections between cultures

By choosing Honduras Volunteer, you’re not just signing up for a volunteer program – you’re joining a community committed to positive change and cultural understanding.
